METROLINK

  • I'm not use to riding on a railway system, how does it work?

    • The local railway system is called the MetroLink. There are multiple trains which depart at various times throughout the day. These trains travel both underground and at street level.

    There are two lines on the MetroLink, red and blue. The two lines start at different locations, but converge to stop at the sames stops as it travels east.



  • How much are the fares?

    • There are many different tickets avaliable for purchase. Popular options are the single-ride ticket which is $2.50 and the 1-day ticket which is $7.50

    If you plan on traveling via metro frequently, consider investing in a weekly or monthly pass. They are $27 and $78, respectively.



  • Can I ride the Metro without a ticket?

    • While there are definitely people who do choose to ride without purchasing a ticket, this would be a bad idea. Metro police conduct random train searches for exactly these types of people. If you are caught riding without a vaild ticket you will be asked to leave the train and will be written a ticket. In many cases, offenders will have to attend court and/or pay a fine.

    It's awkward when you get caught doing something that was wrong, so don't do it. Save yourself the trouble and just buy the ticket.

TAXI & UBER

  • Taxi

    • Just like many other major cities, St. Louis has many taxi companies. But if you are from a place like New York, don't come here expecting the taxi services to be the same.

    There will not be a large number of taxis just waiting around for their next customer, you are probably won't be able to hail a taxi here like you see in the movies. Unless you are at a typical taxi money making spot like the airport, you will need to call and schedule your pick upi time.

    Many taxi companies now also have apps that you can download which expedites the ordering process.



  • Does St. Louis have Uber?

    • As of late 2015, Uber joined the St. Louis area. Since its start, Uber has provided thousands of ride to people living in St. Louis.

    Make sure you download the app so you can take advantage of getting discounts on your first ride.
